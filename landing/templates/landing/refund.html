Refund Policy

Updated April 10th, 2021

We hope you’ll find Kanaboard useful on your journey to medical school. However, we understand if it’s not quite what you expected. Below are some examples of how we can refund you.

If you purchased a monthly or yearly subscription to Kanaboard, but decided within two months it’s just not for you (on top of the free trial), we’ll give you a full refund.

If you have a situation that doesn’t apply to the above, let us know, and we’ll look into giving you a partial refund.

We know how challenging this journey is, which is why we’ll do everything we can to help you along the way. If you have any questions or concerns, reach out to us at refund@kanaboard.com, and we’ll do our best to make things right.
