Who is Kanaboard for?

Kanaboard was built for any pre-med student planning on attending medical school. Whether you're a freshman or a non-traditional student, Kanaboard will help you plan and stay on track wherever you are in the pre-med process.

I use Excel to keep track of my application. How is Kanaboard different?

Kanaboard makes it easy to assign tasks to each area of your application, so you'll always know what to work on. It also asks the exact information you'll need for your application, so you can copy everything directly from your Kanaboard account when it's time to apply.
